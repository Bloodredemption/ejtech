BSIT: Best Course for Every Computer Enthusiast

This course builds a person who is flexible and adaptable problem solver who is ready and willing to learn about working with systems and with people.

EJ Lester B. Pimentel February 25, 2022 · Admin
Share this

Hello everyone! My name is EJ Lester B. Pimentel, I'm currently a first year BSIT student.

Let's start with knowing on what is the BSIT course. Base on the internet, the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) is a bachelor's degree awarded for an undergraduate program in the information technology. The degree is normally required in order to work in the Information technology industry. The degree program typically takes three to four years depending on the country. This degree is primarily focused on subjects such as software, databases, and networking.

This course builds a person who is flexible and adaptable problem solver who is ready and willing to learn about working with systems and with people.

IT majors focus on how information and computing systems support business, research, and communications needs. Instruction ranges from the basics of computer hardware to the complex relationship between humans and computers.

Just like other people who has the same interests like mine, I also want to be part of IT Industry and do things better and cooler but first I need to practice my skills everyday so that I can be proficient in my dream job someday with the help of this course I can gain the necessary skills to solve challenges through data analysis.

The BSIT course is perfect for me because I'm a computer enthusiast and I love doing things related to computer and stuffs. My dream job is to become a software engineer or a full-stack developer. I know it is hard from the very start but I'll continue working hard to achieve my dream job.

The Dream Job I Want

Many students ends up with a speciality. Meaning to say, IT major students after they graduate, they must pick a track in one area of interest. So for me, I pick full-stack developer. Being a full-stack developer is hard. A full stack web developer is a person who can develop both client and server software. Basically, I need to practice the following:

  • Program a browser (like using JavaScript, jQuery, Angular, or Vue)
  • Program a server (like using PHP, ASP, Python, or Node)
  • Program a database (like using SQL, SQLite, or MongoDB)

It's a tough one but I need to practice every programming language needed for that specific role which is the full-stack developer.

Being a full-stack developer also has advantages and disadvantages. The advantages of being a full-stack developer is you can master all the techniques involved in  a development project. It can reduce the time used for communication, meeting, and other stuffs. You can also switch from frontend to backend development so you can provide help to other team members. Also it has disadvantages, one is that being a full-stack developer is increasingly complex and the solution chosen can be wrong for the project. To be honest, I make mistake sometimes and it is normal. What's not normal is other people making the same mistake all over again so we must learn from it and not do it again.

I've seen people on the internet who has the job I want and I've taken notes on what to do. As I watched them do cool things it made me realized one thing and that is to do better. For the sake of my future and my family.
