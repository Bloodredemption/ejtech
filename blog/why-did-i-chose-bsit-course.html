Why did I chose BSIT course?

Hesitating on choosing what course should I get.

Augustine Bhern A. Dumapias February 23, 2022 · Author
Share this

Ever since I'm in elementary days, I've play a lots of computer games and later that I realize that it becomes my hobby like I want to know the basic of computer parts and building my own computer if I become a successful someday. That hobby of mine is already within me when I graduated in elementary into high school students.

That time, I continue to learn all about the basic parts of computer because there's a computer subject in our class and it helps me a lot. When I graduated into senior high students, it was required that we should choose what strand to get and I decided that IT is best for me and I chose it. I've learned a lot of it like knowing what are the names of the basic parts of computer, learning how to disassemble and assemble of a system unit, how to install Windows XP and Windows 7 in computer, and learning to make signal cables.

When I graduated into senior high, there's a time that my family gather in one place and they asked me what course should I choose in college, I tell them that I already chose BSIT because it is what I want to get but my family argued about it that I should choose another course like Criminology because it was way better for me, that moment I've been thinking a lot on what should I choose a course in my college days. Later that day, my mother told me that "I should choose whatever course I want that will makes me happy, always remember that we're here for me", that moment I've felt very happy to hear that from my mom and I realize that I should choose BSIT course.

Choosing a BSIT course is a hard part

When I begin on embarking my journey as a BSIT student on my college days, it's already hard for me from the start even for my classmates because we've encountered a new discussions like coding on C programming using Codeblocks, Dcoder and etc. using personal computer and cell phone. We can't learn enough because we're facing COVID-19 pandemic nowadays and the DEPED decided to make a new learning method on online class to the fact that the Philippines was one of the slowest internet connection in the world according to the research. As for now, I'm starting on adjusting to the new learning method because I'm not used to this kind of situation, but I'm still thankful that somehow I can catch up to the discussions, activities, and reporting so far and I hope that my classmates will also catch up and adjusting well to this new learning method.

Therefore I conclude that whatever the challenges that I'll be facing someday, I will never give up because I know that God will guide me to the right path and I'm willing to learn more about to this world because there are so many things that I don't know yet and I'm gonna do my very best to make my family, and friends proud and to be acknowledge by everyone to my efforts someday. I will get this degree someday no matter what happens. Thank you!

I will give my gratitude to my family and friends who supports me on what I'm capable of doing this days and without them, I can't make it here on where am I right now. God bless!
